The Origin

Built From
A Real Need

There's a generation of creators moving between cities, time zones, and continents — building companies from hotel rooms, airports, and open-air markets. They carry everything but still arrive nowhere with a proper workspace.

"You can travel anywhere. But your workspace cannot follow."

That was the frustration at the heart of Joule. As an African creator building across borders, OLO Art Studio felt it firsthand — the friction of working on the move, the hunched posture, the scattered setup.

So they stopped adapting to broken systems — and built a new one.
